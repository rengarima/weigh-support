Getting started
What is Weigh for?Getting started

A calm daily weight tracker. Step on the scale, tap the number, watch the trend. Weigh quietly correlates your weigh-ins with sleep, cycle, calendar events, and meals so the trend is readable — not just a wobbly line.

Why are calories called "estimates"?Getting started

Photo-based calorie counting is a starting point, not a measurement. The model guesses portion size from a 2D image — it gets dish names right far more often than the calorie count. Edit the number after the estimate comes back if you know better.

Why does my goal recommendation use BMR × activity?Getting started

Mifflin-St Jeor BMR multiplied by an activity factor is the most widely-validated quick formula. It's a fine starting point. After two or three weeks of weigh-ins, your trend will tell you whether the number needs to come up or down.

Weigh-ins
What's the best time to weigh in?Weigh-ins

Same time, same scale, fasted, after the bathroom. The exact number matters less than the consistency — a stable routine lets the 7-day average tell you something real.

Why does the dial round to 0.1?Weigh-ins

Most home scales can't reliably resolve below 100 grams. Rounding keeps the trend honest and stops you over-reading day-to-day noise.

How do I delete a weigh-in?Weigh-ins

Open Calendar from the side drawer, tap the day, then tap the trash icon in the entry detail. Seed/demo entries (when seed mode is on) are display-only and reappear when the list re-renders.

Food, photos & chat
How does the photo meal log work?Food, photos & chat

Tap Food → Take Photo. Snap a meal or pick from your library. Add a note like "no dressing" or "double portion" if it helps — the model adjusts. Confirm the photo, wait a few seconds, and you get a dish name, calories, and macros you can edit before saving.

How accurate are photo estimates?Food, photos & chat

Roughly ±20% on calories from a single photo. The model is much better at naming the dish than judging the portion. Edit the kcal number if you know it; user-edited entries also help the model calibrate future similar meals.

Why is there a daily cap on photos and chats?Food, photos & chat

AI calls cost real money on our end. Free covers light use (3 photos and 5 chats per day). Plus lifts both to 20 and 50. Pro to 100 and 200. The badge on Food and Chat shows what you've used today.

What can I ask the chat?Food, photos & chat

Anything about your own data — "how was my sleep last week?", "is my weight trending up?", "did dinners out hit my weigh-in?". The model only sees what you can already see in the app, summarized as numbers.

Does the chat see my name or personal details?Food, photos & chat

No. The chat receives a summary of your data — weight trend, sleep stages, cycle phase, recent meals, calendar events — without name, email, or identifiers. Nothing trains a model.

How do I delete a meal?Food, photos & chat

On the Food page, swipe a meal entry left or tap the trash icon in its detail. Demo entries (seed mode on) are display-only.

Plans & subscriptions
What's the difference between Free, Plus, and Pro?Plans & subscriptions

Free gives you 3 photo meal logs and 5 chat turns a day. Plus lifts that to 20 photos and 50 chats. Pro goes to 100 photos and 200 chats — useful if you log every meal or chat a lot. Everything else (weight, cycle, sleep, hydration, insights) is identical across tiers.

How do I upgrade?Plans & subscriptions

Settings → Subscription → Upgrade. Or tap any plan badge across the app. Or wait until you hit the daily cap — the paywall opens automatically with the upgrade options.

How do I cancel?Plans & subscriptions

iOS handles this — open the system Settings app → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Weigh → Cancel. Your tier stays active until the current period ends.

Is there a free trial?Plans & subscriptions

Annual plans (Plus Annual, Pro Annual) include a 7-day free trial for first-time subscribers. Cancel within the trial and you're not charged.

Can I switch between Plus and Pro?Plans & subscriptions

Yes, anytime. iOS prorates the upgrade automatically. Settings → Subscription → tap the other plan.

What if I run out of photos today?Plans & subscriptions

The Take Photo button greys out with an "Upgrade to scan" prompt. Caps reset at UTC midnight. Manual entry and barcode scanning stay available regardless of plan.

Cycle, sleep & health
How do I turn on cycle tracking?Cycle, sleep & health

Open Settings → set Gender to Female under Profile → toggle Show on home → Cycle on. The Cycle tab will appear in navigation, alongside the cycle card on Home.

How do I connect Apple Health?Cycle, sleep & health

Open Settings → Connect → Apple Health. iOS will ask which data types you want to share. You can change this later in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Weigh.

Where does my sleep data come from?Cycle, sleep & health

Apple Health. Weigh reads sleep stages with your permission and shows a per-night hypnogram, weekly stage averages, and a low-deep-sleep detector. We never write sleep back to Health.

What do the calendar dots mean?Cycle, sleep & health

Up to five small markers under each day: sage = weight logged, rose = period day, terracotta = event, gold = workout, purple = at least one meal photo logged. Legend lives below the month grid.

Privacy & data
Where does my data live?Privacy & data

On your device. Weight, cycle, meal, sleep, and craving logs are stored locally in iOS Documents. Apple Health data syncs through HealthKit with your permission.

What data leaves my device?Privacy & data

Only what's needed for the photo and chat features: the image (for photo log) or your typed question + a summary of your data (for chat) goes to our AI proxy. No name, no email, no Health raw stream. Nothing trains a model. See the privacy policy for the full statement.

Does Weigh have a server?Privacy & data

A small one — just to route AI calls and enforce the daily caps. Weight, meals, sleep, and cycle data never touch it. Subscription state is the only thing we keep server-side, keyed on an anonymous ID.

Does Weigh sync between devices?Privacy & data

Not yet. Right now your data lives on the device you logged it on. Apple Health entries you write from Weigh will sync via iCloud Health like any other Health data.

Will Weigh ever send me notifications?Privacy & data

Only the daily reminder, if you enable it in Settings. Weigh doesn't send marketing or engagement nudges.

How do I delete my account?Privacy & data

Settings → Account → Delete account. This wipes your subscription record server-side and removes everything from this device. Apple Health entries stay in Health — iOS owns those, and you can delete them in the Health app.
