Common questions

Where does my data live?

On your iPhone, and in Apple Health if you grant Health permission. Weigh has no servers, no account, and no analytics. Nothing leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

How do I turn on cycle tracking?

Open Settings (gear tab) → set Gender to Female → toggle Cycle tracking on. The Cycle tab will appear in navigation.

How do I connect Apple Health?

Open Settings → Connect → tap Apple Health. iOS will ask which data types you want to share. You can change this later in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Weigh.

How do I delete an entry?

Tap any day in the Calendar tab to see entries for that day, then tap the trash icon next to any entry.

Does Weigh sync between devices?

Not yet. Right now your data lives on the device you logged it on. Apple Health entries you write from Weigh will sync via iCloud Health like any other Health data.

Will Weigh ever send me notifications?

Only the daily reminder, if you enable it in Settings. Weigh doesn't send marketing or engagement nudges.

Privacy

Weigh does not collect, transmit, or sell any user data. There is no networking code in the app. See the privacy policy for the full statement.
